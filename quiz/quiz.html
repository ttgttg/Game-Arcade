Quiz!

Test yourself with these questions...

    There is a 4 strawberry card on the deck. To ring the bell how many strawberries should be on the card that will put down?

    What should you do if you ring the bell wrong?

    If the other player put down 1 banana card and you put down 5 bananas card, should you ring the bell?

    If two players ring the bell at the same time but player 1's hand is below player 2, will player 1 receive the point?

    When you put down 3 strawberry card, which card should you put down next to ring the bell?
