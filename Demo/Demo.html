Demo Halli Galli

Have you learned the rules of Halli Galli enough? If so, let's practice in a Demo game! 🎮​
On this page, you can experience a demo of the Halli Galli game. When you press the start button, the card will flip after 3 seconds, and the game will begin.
When the cards satisfy the Halli Galli rules, simply press the bell in front of you. 🛎️​
If you press the button first after the cards are flipped, you will earn 1 point, while if your opponent presses the bell first, he will earn 1 point.
Once the outcome is decided, it's the end of one round. So if you want to play again, simply press the start button again!​ 📟​
