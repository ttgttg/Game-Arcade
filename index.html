BRIEF GAME DESCRIPTION



  • Each player turns over a card in the order of play.
  • Soon as the revealed cards show exactly 5 fruits of the same sort, the first player to ring the bell wins all of the revealed cards.
  • The aim of the game is to win the largest number of cards.
