Game: Unpacking by Witch Beam Studio

Your Ultimate Guide to Unpacking

An interactive story about unpacking a life...

Unpacking is a captivating journey through the mundane yet deeply personal act of unpacking belongings and fitting them into a new home. In short, the game invites you to organise and decorate various living spaces over the course of several years, and in its duration, uncover a narrative that unfolds through the context of these possessions. You get to experience a sense of intimacy with a character you never see, and a story you're never told in words.

The zen gameplay evokes a rich tapestry of human experience and connection, prompting players to consider the emotional significance of everyday objects and the memories they hold.

Beginner's Guide

1. Getting Started

  • Start by downloading and installing the game on your preferred platform. Unpacking is available on PC, iOS, Android, PlayStation, XBox, and Nintendo Switch!
  • Launch the game and create a new save.

2. Understanding the Interface

  • Familiarize yourself with the game's interface. You'll see a room with boxes scattered around.
  • The goal of each level is to empty all of the boxes.
  • Initially clicking on a box will open it. Clicking again will remove an item which then must be placed somewhere.
  • The paper inside the box will slowly reduce as you remove items.

3. Organising Items

  • Take your time to carefully consider where each item should go. The game doesn't rush you, so enjoy the process!
  • Some items have obvious places to go, like putting clothes in a wardrobe or books on a bookshelf.
  • Items aren't always packaged in the right room. You may have to switch between different rooms to put them in the right place.

4. Accessibility Settings

  • Unpacking offers accessibility options such as colorblind mode and customizable controls. Feel free to adjust these settings to suit your preferences.

Ready to Take it Further?
Here are some extra tips to help you succeed!

  1. Explore the Space First
    • Take some time to have a look around the entire house, open every drawer, and get a good idea of what spaces are available.
    • You will discover hidden storage spaces by interacting with existing items.
  2. Items Can't Be Placed Randomly
    • Once every box has been opened and every item has been placed, incorrectly arranged ones will flash red.
    • You should take the time to properly inspect every item and think logically about where most people would place corresponding items in their house.
  3. Collect the Stickers!
    • Certain interactions will trigger achievements, rewarding you with stickers that you can decorate the scrapbook with.
    • Try clicking an item after placing it down! For example, turning on the TV.
  4. Try Seeing the Full Picture First
    • When unpacking boxes, we don't know which item will come next.
    • Therefore, sometimes it's a much better idea to empty the entire contents of a box onto the floor and then start moving the items around after seeing everything that you will need to organize.
  5. Dark Star Mode
    • Dark Star is a secret game-mode in Unpacking.
    • Once every item is incorrectly placed they will all stop glowing red and a black star will glow in the bottom right corner.
    • After the Dark Star appears in the right corner of a room the level-complete music is different. Any music played by an item is also distorted and slowed...
